Orange Chicken Recipe

Description

Budget friendly 4-ingredient orange chicken, good with rice.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup marmalade
  • 1/2 cup BBQ sauce
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 pound chicken nuggets

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400f/200c and place nuggets on baking sheet.
  2. Bake until hot and crispy, follow package directions.
  3. Whisk marmalade, BBQ sauce, and soy sauce in small saucepan on low until hot.
  4. Place nuggets in a bowl and cover with suace.
