Chili Recipe

Description

An easy 5-ingredient chili that is quick and simple to make.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 15 ounces tomato sauce
  • 15 ounce of kidney beans
  • 15 ounce of chili beans
  • 2 tablespoons of chili powder

Directions

  1. Heat large skillet over medium head, cook ground beef until browned to crumbly.
  2. Stir in the rest of the ingredients and bring to a boil.
  3. Reduce heat to simmer and stir occasionally until thickened.
